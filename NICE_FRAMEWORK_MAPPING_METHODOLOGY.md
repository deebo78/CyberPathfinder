# NICE Framework Career Track Mapping Methodology

## Overview

This document explains how the 19 cybersecurity career tracks in CyberPathfinder were mapped to official NICE Framework Work Roles, establishing Tasks, Knowledge, and Skills (TKS) inheritance at each career progression level.

## Mapping Strategy

### Core Principles

1. **Natural Career Progression**: Each level builds logically on the previous, moving from tactical execution to strategic leadership
2. **NICE Category Diversity**: Progression spans across NICE categories to reflect growing responsibility scope
3. **Authentic Competency Requirements**: All TKS requirements are inherited directly from official NICE Framework Work Roles
4. **Industry-Aligned Advancement**: Mappings reflect real-world cybersecurity career advancement patterns

### Progression Philosophy

- **Entry-Level**: Hands-on technical execution within specific domains
- **Mid-Level**: Broader system analysis and specialized implementation
- **Senior-Level**: Process oversight, compliance, and team leadership
- **Expert-Level**: Strategic architecture, enterprise design, and organizational influence

## Implementation Benefits

1. **Evidence-Based Career Planning**: Each progression level has concrete, official competency requirements
2. **Skills Gap Identification**: Clear visibility into required vs. current capabilities
3. **Training Program Alignment**: Educational content can map directly to NICE Framework requirements
4. **Industry Standardization**: Career paths align with nationally recognized cybersecurity framework
5. **Recruitment Clarity**: Hiring managers have specific competency benchmarks for each level

## Validation and Maintenance

The mapping methodology was validated through:
- Cross-reference with industry career progression patterns
- Alignment with NICE Framework category definitions
- Review of TKS requirement complexity and appropriateness for each level
- Verification of logical progression across career levels

Mappings are maintained to reflect:
- Updates to NICE Framework Work Role definitions
- Evolution of cybersecurity career market demands
- Feedback from industry practitioners and hiring managers
